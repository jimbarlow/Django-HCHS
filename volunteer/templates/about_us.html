This is the internal volunteers' portal for the Hart County Humane Society


Hart County Humane Society is an all-volunteer organization with no paid employees
Our mission is to make Hart and Franklin Counties "no-kill" counties for dogs and cats.
We do this through our loc cost Spay/Neuter program, and by our adoption program for dogs that we rescue from the North East Georgia Animal Shelter
We encourage you to join us, and you can apply to volunteer by clicking on the "Apply to Volunteer" link below
